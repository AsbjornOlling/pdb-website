High-fidelity speaker drivers

Hector uses 6 full-size hi-fi drivers from Dayton Audio. The four woofers and two tweeters are divided into two discrete cabinets, in order to eliminate destructive interference within the speaker.




110dB @ 10THD

So, how loud is it? Only one way to say it: Hector goes LOUD. A regular vacuum cleaner is about 70dB. Roskilde Festival allows up to 100dB for their concerts. Hector can easily play 110dB.




Optimal linear response

Hector uses the powerful Class-D Max-amp from Accusafe, along with a pair of second order crossover filters, at 12dB/oct, making the sound extremely unbiased across frequencies.
