Old-school tactile switches

Hector uses pleasantly tactile switches and knobs. Every flipswitch has a satisfying feel, and the volume knob is endlessly smooth. All of them are made entirely of metal, and are labelled with laser-engraved symbols in the wood.



Complementary screw colors

Any color of finish you choose will come with a set of screws in a complementary color; Chrome screws for the light stain, gold screws for the dark stain, black screws for the black painted.



guitar strap

Hector comes with a high quality synthetic leather guitar strap, making it wearable for easy portability. The all-metal strap hooks make the strap easily removable and/or replacable. Placement of the strap has been carefully calulated for ergonomic comfort.
